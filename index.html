About Superhero Burgers

Founded in 2018 by comic book enthusiast and master chef Alex Rivera, Superhero Burgers began as a small food truck with big dreams. Combining a passion for gourmet burgers with a love for superhero lore, we've created a unique dining experience that's truly heroic.

Our annual festival brings together fans of great food and superhero culture for three days of delicious fun. All our burgers are made with locally-sourced ingredients and packed with flavor that would make even Thor's hammer drop!
